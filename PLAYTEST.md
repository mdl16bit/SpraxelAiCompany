## 0. Master keybinding cheat-sheet (print this)

**Movement:** A/D move · W/S up-down (climb/swim) · **Space** jump · **Shift** sprint · **C** duck / cover / slide · **=`/`−`/wheel** zoom
**Universal actions:** **E** interact · **Z** extract (on extraction point) · **T** coin · **Y** EMP · **U** gas · **Shift+U** flare · **Shift+W** water flask · **Shift+Y** flashbang · **G** grab/drag · **V** tag · **R** push · **J** drop gear · **K** knock wall · **H** drill door · **B** pistol (hold to aim) · **\\** play dead · **`** / **0** use item / held item · **M** call coordinator · **I** sniper scope · **X** toggle vision cones
**Abilities (archetype-specific):** **Q** = ability 1 · **F** = ability 2 · **R** = ability 3 · **Z** = ability 4
**Squad:** **1–8** select operative · **PageUp/PageDown** switch next/prev · **N** cycle most-recent · **Tab (hold)** Plan Mode · **left-click** queue order in plan mode · **Alt+click** queue Plan-B fallback route (Plan Mode) · **Ctrl+Enter** arm synchronized multi-entry breach (Plan Mode) · **right-click teammate** → Go Loud diversion
**Menus:** **Esc** pause/back · **F11** or **P** fullscreen · **.** minimap toggle · **M** (on Results) open Post-Mission Crew Report

> ⚠️ Several keys are **context-sensitive** (G, R, V, Z, C, do different things depending on what's nearby / plan-mode / scope). If a binding "does nothing," check your context and that the right archetype is selected.

## 2. Core Movement & Platforming  *(test in Warehouse Job)*
- **Move / walk** — A/D (left-right), W/S (climb/swim axis).
- **Jump** — **Space**. Has coyote time, jump buffer, variable height (release early to cut), multi-jump for Acrobat.
- **Sprint** — hold **Shift** while moving. 1.5× speed, drains stamina → ~1.5 s catch-breath when exhausted. *Sprinting 2 s+ marks you "scented" for dogs.*
- **Duck / crouch** — hold **C**. Lowers detection, crawl when moving, blocked under low ceilings. **C** also takes cover next to a cover-prop.
- **Slide** — **C** while sprinting (needs `perk_slide` + stamina). ~0.7 s burst, can't steer/jump mid-slide.
- **Wall-press flatten** — hold into a wall: flattens silhouette ~50%, drops detection. *(No classic wall-jump.)*
- **Ladder climb** — W/S on a ladder; **Space** to jump off.
- **Swim** — automatic in water zones (**Docks**); W/S dive/surface, oxygen + drag + auto-surface.
- **Rope/cable/zipline/ledge-hang/pull-up/deployable kits/shoulder-boost** — context traversal; test at rope anchors (**Vault**) and cables (**Foundry**).
- **Camera zoom** — **=** / **−** / mouse wheel.

## 3. Characters / Archetypes & Abilities
**How it works:** all operatives share one base; behavior differs by **archetype** (43 enum values, ~26 with a keyed ability module). **You can only test an archetype's abilities if you deploy it** — pick a varied squad in Briefing. **Ability keys: Q = ability 1, F = 2, R = 3, Z = 4** (default cooldowns 3/8/5/5 s).

Notable archetypes (slot → key):
- **Hacker (Vex)** — **Q** context hack (phone-tap freeze 6 s / hack door — *schedule-corrupt removed 2026-07-11, folded into Route-marker*), **F** loop a camera. Terminal hacks via **E**. **V** Route-marker relocate — now THE sole cursor-directed guard-relocate tool. *(2026-07-11, T-279d)* **4-tool core formalized**: camera loop / timed terminal hack / keypad camera replay / Route-marker are Hacker-locked; **fusebox, splice box, patrol-schedule terminal, and architect scan are now gated behind the Electronics skill branch** for everyone else (T1 = fusebox/schedule-terminal/phone-tap, T2 = splice-box/architect-scan). A non-eligible character pressing E on a gated object gets a denied SFX + `hacker.tool_restricted` trace, and its HUD prompt stays hidden. Multi-hacker co-op terminals remain universal. *Test:* walk the Bruiser (no Electronics) to a fusebox and press E → denied buzz, no prompt; same spot with Vex → normal hack. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=hacker-core-toolkit` (alias of `hacker-balance`).
- **Acrobat (Mire)** — **Q** blind-spot vault / ledge-yank, **F** grapple, **R** *(new 2026-06-24)* Partner Hoist Line — aim at a wall/ledge above and press R to lift the nearest teammate (within 96 px) up to it in ~0.8 s (10 s cooldown); distinct from shoulder-carry (grapple-range, cursor-aimed vs. close-range hold-E). **Z** aerial dive-kick. Extra air-jumps. *First test:* Warehouse Job — stand Mire near Vex, aim at the upper floor, press R. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=acrobat-partner-hoist`.
- **Bruiser (Halcyon)** — **Q** silent takedown of a nearby unaware guard; **hold F** riot-shield block / **tap F** chained takedowns; **hold R** body hurl. *(Needed for Foundry.)*
- **Sniper / Marksman (Wren)** — **Q** rangefinder, **F** auto-tag, **Z** suppression shot. Scope = **I**.
- **Medic (Sable)** — **Q** revive/stim, **F** antidote/sedative trap/heal, **Z** ranged tranq dart (1-shot KO).
- **Pickpocket (Pip)** — **Q** lift loot/keys, **F** sedative trap.
- **Demolitionist (Cinder)** — **Q** place charge, **F** detonate + scent pack, **R** decoy/grease, **Z** flare→smoke. *(Needed for Foundry hatch.)*
- **Disguiser (Veil)** — **Q** forge ID / crowd-loiter, **F** refresh disguise *(2026-07-11: impersonation-call removed, folded into Route-marker; F now = the Refresh Disguise ability Imposter uses)*. - **Ghost (Echo)** — **Q** background-blend cloak (sprint breaks it).

- **Kit-Charge Economy** *(new 2026-06-24, 3-part epic)* — every character now shares a **KIT** charge pool (top-right HUD meter, `KIT <cur>/<max>` with a cyan→yellow→red bar) that fuels throwables (coin 10, EMP 25) *and* every Q/F/R/Z slot ability (guard-manipulation and hacker tools each have a distinct cost). ## 4. Character Switching · Squad Control · Plan Mode
- **Select operative 1–8** — number keys **1**–**8** (downed operatives skipped).
- **Switch next / prev** — **PageUp / PageDown**.
- **Cycle most-recently-used** — **N**. *Switching fires a "swap radar ping" that briefly reveals nearby guards.*
- **Plan Mode** — **hold Tab** to slow time and queue orders (`scripts/systems/plan_mode.gd`). While held:
  - **Left-click** = queue a move/interact/takedown for the active operative.
  - **Z** cycle start-delay · **X** clear this queue · **Shift+X** clear all queues.
  - **B** chain tagged takedowns · **O** overwatch · **L** lookout · **Shift+T** sync · **G** drop decoy · **,** wait-for-signal · **.** go-signal.
  - **Tab+Backspace** Panic Recall (abort choreography) · **[** / **]** scrub queued plan back/forward.
  - **Alt+click** *(new 2026-06-21)* — queue a **Plan-B fallback route** for the active character (dashed orange line alongside the solid primary path). On release, the character auto-pivots to the fallback if any watching guard's detection reaches ≥80% or an ALERTED/SUSPICIOUS guard sits within 120 px of the next waypoint (fires at most once per release). *Test:* Warehouse Job — queue a primary route, Alt+click a safer alternate, release Tab near a guard. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=plan-b-route`.
  - **Ctrl+Enter** *(new 2026-06-21)* — arm a **Synchronized Multi-Entry Breach** while 2+ characters have queued INTERACT/HACK/TAKEDOWN. On release, a 3-2-1 countdown appears over each armed character and all fire on the same tick; guard radios are jammed for ~1 s so they can't warn each other. A clean sync (no prior alert, 2+ chars) scores a **+400 bonus** on Results. *Test:* queue two thieves on separate doors/guards, Ctrl+Enter, release Tab. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=sync-breach`.
  - Release **Tab** to execute. Watch the **PlanMode / Executing / PlanCooldown** HUD labels.
- **World Notes** *(new 2026-07-17, narrative)* — readable props (memos, terminal logs, clippings). **E** to read → one-line lore subtitle via the dialogue popup; single-use per mission (prompt disappears after). Debrief shows a quiet "NOTES: N read" row on win and loss. Per-level lines drop in at `assets/dialogue/world_notes_<level>.json`. *Test:* Warehouse Job — the ForemanMemo on the foreman's desk (JSON-pool text); Office Hours ServerLog (in-code fallback text). *Debug:* `--demo-feature=world-notes`.
- **Crew Nerve** *(new 2026-07-02, passive, no HUD meter)* — witnessing a squadmate get KO'd, taking a hostile hit, or a full alarm rattles a thief's composure; below 50% ("rattled") their queued Plan Mode step fires ~0.2–0.4 s late. Standing near a Bonded partner recovers nerve 3× faster. No number is ever shown — you only feel it as "my queue felt laggy" after losing a teammate. *Test:* let a squadmate get KO'd in your line of sight, then immediately Plan-Mode-queue the witness — note the slight delay on execute. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=crew-nerve`.

## 5. Stealth · Detection · AI · Alarms  *(Warehouse for basics, Vault for GHOST)*
- **Toggle vision cones** — **X** (best playtest aid; also shows guard ghost-paths).
- **Detection states** — guard FSM PATROL → SUSPICIOUS → SEARCHING → ALERTED → KO/DEAD (+ CHATTING, FATIGUED, LURED, TIED_UP, STUNNED, ESCORT — *SCHEDULE_CORRUPT state removed 2026-07-11 with the schedule-corruption tool*). Guard types: Patroller, Responder, Civilian, Heavy, K9, Riot, Detective, Checkpoint; 5 skill levels Rookie→Spec-Ops.

> ✂️ **Tool consolidation (2026-07-11, T-7abb — epic complete):** five guard-manipulation tools were removed/folded — Patrol Schedule Corruption + Guard Impersonation Call → **Route-marker (V)**; Stolen Walkie-Talkie → **Radio Wire Cut** (sole silence tool; Pickpocket no longer starts with a walkie); Decoy Prop (Z) → Disguiser **passive**; Pinned Note → gone (Charmer Song / Sound Decoy cover in-place distraction). The three survivors are now **archetype-owned**: relocate = Route-marker (**Hacker**, or Electronics-L1), silence = Radio Wire Cut (**Pickpocket**), guard-vs-guard = Distraction Radio Chatter (**Disguiser** — even a Hacker with the full skill chain gets a denied SFX now). *Test:* try Radio Wire Cut with a non-Pickpocket next to a guard → denied buzz + trace; same for chatter with a non-Disguiser. If an old recipe below mentions a removed tool, the survivor is the tool to reach for.
- **Caught-state HUD** — top-right **GREEN ◆ CLEAN / YELLOW ◆ SPOTTED / RED ◆ CAUGHT** (ratchets up only). **VIS** darkness bar bottom-left.
- **Three detection axes** — **sight** (wall-clipped cones, dimmed by dark/duck/disguise), **hearing** (loudness pulses, wall-attenuated), **smell** (sprint/gas/flagged-loot → "scented"; dogs always smell — wash off in water or stand still ~20 s).
- **Detection modes (per mission)** — LENIENT (score only), RESOLVE (return to CLEAN before extracting — Rooftop), GHOST (any detection soft-fails — Vault).
- **Alarms** — **Guard Radio Countdown** (12 s ring on ALERTED — KO or make 350+ loudness within 120 px to cancel), **Civilian Phone Call** (~11 s — KO or charm to stop), **Alarm Panel** (smash/hack silences a zone 60 s), heavy reinforcement waves on full alarm.
- **Hero Vendetta** *(new 2026-07-17)* — KO a named hero (Reyes in Warehouse Job / Office Hours) and she permanently remembers that thief's face: her next spawn starts SUSPICIOUS, and that one thief's disguise never works on her again ("I remember that face" codec fires once on first recognition). Other thieves' disguises still work; other guards unaffected; survives save/load. *Test:* KO Reyes with the Acrobat in Warehouse, finish, then play Office Hours disguised as the Acrobat and walk into her sightline. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=hero-vendetta`.
- **Turncoat** *(new 2026-07-11)* — 4th interrogation step: after a tied-up guard has given up intel (KO → tie → closet → interrogate), press **E** on the same guard again → 6 s gold "Flipping..." channel → for 60 s his radio feeds false all-clears: radio countdowns on guards within 300 px keep cancelling and civilian phone-call dispatch is suppressed in that zone. A "📻 TURNCOAT" tag floats over him; he stays tied; killing him ends the effect. *Test:* Warehouse Job supply closet — interrogate, flip, then deliberately get half-spotted nearby and watch the alerted guard's radio ring die. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=turncoat`.
- **Guard Counter-trap** *(new 2026-07-11)* — a SUSPICIOUS (not alerted) Patroller/Responder at guard-level 2+ near a Supply Closet marker has a 40% chance to break off, kneel 3 s ("Arming trap…" telegraph), and leave a visible orange tripwire at the chokepoint, then resume patrol. Trips characters only (1 s stagger + alert bump); guards walk through it. **Hold E** within 48 px for 1.5 s to disarm. *Test:* Warehouse Job upper floor — let GuardA half-spot you, back off, watch him jog to the closet; then step over or disarm the wire. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=guard-counter-trap`.
- **Guard Verbal Challenge** *(new 2026-06-24)* — a half-spotting guard (real line-of-sight glimpse crossing SUSPICIOUS) barks "Hey — you!" and freezes for 3 s. Break line-of-sight or **Bluff** (below) within the window or they escalate to ALERTED and radio backup. Does **not** fire for radio/body/sound-driven suspicion. *Test:* walk into a patroller's cone in Warehouse Job. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=guard-challenge`.
- **Bluff** *(new 2026-06-24, disguise-gated)* — press **Q** near a SUSPICIOUS/SEARCHING guard (requires disguise or a fake ID) to open a timed QTE bar; press **Q** again in the green zone to talk them back to PATROL (detection→0). Miss the window and they go ALERTED with no refund. 3 composure charges per mission; at 0 the prompt reads "(rattled)". *Debug:* `--demo-feature=bluff`.
- **Guard Surrender** *(new 2026-06-24)* — an isolated ALERTED guard (no live buddy in radio range) confronted at close range (~64 px) by an armed Bruiser or Sniper throws hands up — frozen, no radio, no detection. **E** to tie up, or **R** to drag + **E** to stash in a Closet, without a takedown animation. Breaks if a second guard enters radio range or you back off past 64 px. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=guard-surrender`.
- **Silent Alarm (Panic Button)** *(new 2026-07-02, no player affordance)* — some guard posts hide a desk duress button; if that guard so much as goes SUSPICIOUS of you, they silently trip it — no HUD tell, no sound. 30 s later a quiet 2-guard reinforcement pair converges on the zone, still walking ordinary PATROL (never shows as "alerted"). Staying fully unseen near a marked guard is the only counter. *First test:* Office Hours lobby reception guard. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=silent-alarm`.
- **Personal Leverage** *(new 2026-07-02)* — some guards carry ONE personal secret (e.g. "THE LEDGER"); a Pickpocket or Disguiser standing near them passively lifts it (same range/timing as badge/key lifts). Any character holding it presses **E** within 64 px of that *specific* guard to make them look away and stop noticing anything for 10 s — no alert, no loot cost. Spending near the wrong guard just plays a denied sound; it's a one-guard, one-use item. *First test:* Warehouse Job — right-click Sentinel-1 (west patrol) to see "Leverage: THE LEDGER" in the dossier, then lift and spend it. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=personal-leverage`.
- **Environmental Tamper Evidence** *(new 2026-07-02, always-on)* — forcing open a locked door (drill/hack/lockpick/shot lock), popping a duct grate, or breaching a wall leaves a "tell" that a PATROL guard within 80 px notices with **zero witness and no body** — goes SUSPICIOUS. 2+ tells within 300 px of each other escalate the discoverer straight to SEARCHING. Tells expire after 180 s if never found. *First test:* drill Warehouse Job's side door or breach the upper-floor glass panel near GuardA's patrol line. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=tamper-tell-evidence`.
- **Guard Weapon Scavenging** *(new 2026-07-02, always-on)* — a KO'd/killed non-civilian guard drops their sidearm. A nearby PATROL partner may notice it (180 px, 0.5 s scan), walk over, and go SUSPICIOUS with detection ×1.4 — no sighting of you needed. Grab it first with **E** for +6 pistol ammo instead. *First test:* KO Radio-Alpha/Bravo near Commander Reyes's patrol in Warehouse Job. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=weapon-scavenging`.
- **Guard Evidence Chain — Coordinated Sweep** *(new 2026-06-21, no player input)* — two body discoveries within 120 s trigger a building-wide 45 s sweep: every non-alerted guard fans out to junction points before resuming patrol. Prevent it by hiding bodies (duct/closet/prop-upright) before discovery. *First test:* KO two guards in Warehouse Job within 2 minutes, leave both exposed. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=guard-evidence-chain`.
- **Emergent behaviors to watch** — guard small-talk, false-search fatigue, end-of-shift naps, **Morale Cascade** (3+ KOs → all PARANOID), shift change (~80 s patrol reversal), buddy checks, wake-up body search, **Countermeasure Adaptation** (guards counter a tactic you overuse), K9 bonds, tension-heartbeat audio.
- **Hero enemy — Commander Reyes** (Elite, disguise-immune) recurs with a codec intro; kill her once and she's gone for good.
- **Campaign Heat** — persistent 0–10 per save; HOT adds guards/faster waves next mission (shown on Mission Select).

HUD warns "◆ STORM FRONT in Xs" then "◆ STORM ACTIVE". Purely passive — no new input. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=storm-front`.
- **Flammable Spill Ignition** *(new 2026-07-02)* — level-authored fuel spills sit inert until a Demolitionist charge blast, a thrown flare (within 32 px), or a stray bullet ignites them. Put it out with a Water Flask (Shift+W, within 120 px) or flood it; burns out on its own after 30 s. *First test:* Warehouse Job's exterior corridor fuel trail (`FuelSpill1`-`4`, between spawn and entrance). *Debug:* `--demo-feature=flammable-spill`.

## 7. Win / Lose · Objectives · Mission Flow · Cutscenes
- **Objectives** — required (bright ✓/○) vs optional side-loot (dim), shown in an **Objective Tracker** with a gold directional arrow + floor tags. **Side Objectives** *(new 2026-06-25)* show at 60% opacity with `[opt] [+¤N]` tags — completing one pays `bonus_payout` into recon budget at mission end; skipping it never blocks the win. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=side-objective`.

- **Lose / soft-fail** — out of time, GHOST detection, or hard-fail flags. Soft-fail → **survival mode** (◆ MISSION FAILED banner + "X safe · Y at risk"): operatives who still extract survive; **downed-and-unrevived = KIA, permanently removed (permadeath).**

Non-hackers get ACCESS DENIED. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=emergency-exit-hack`.
- **Hot Pursuit Phase** *(new 2026-06-21, automatic)* — reaching full alarm (RED caught state) locks all not-yet-hacked doors, doubles heavy-wave frequency (12 s), and starts a 90 s "◆ HOT PURSUIT" countdown top-center. Extract everyone before 0:00 for a **+500 Clean Exfil bonus**; expiry just shows "PURSUIT EXPIRED" (no instant-fail). *Debug:* `--demo-feature=hot-pursuit`.
- **Diversionary Run** *(new 2026-06-21)* — right-click an inactive teammate → "Go Loud (diversion)": they sprint and pulse loud noise every 2.5 s for up to 30 s, pulling nearby PATROL guards. Cancel with **R** while controlling them. Extracting a teammate while the diversion is still running earns the diverter +150 Decoy XP. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=diversion-run`.
- **Throwable Combo (Flare + Smoke)** *(new 2026-06-21)* — throw a smoke grenade (**Z**, Demolitionist), then a flare (**Shift+U**) into the active cloud: a 180 px stun burst zeroes detection on everything in range for 2.5 s, consumes the smoke, and fires a loud 320-loudness pulse. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=throwable-combo`.
- **Mission Role Assignments** *(new 2026-06-24)* — briefing screen ROLES panel (below CARRY ITEM) assigns each thief one of **Pointman** (+50% takedown window if first through a door), **Ghost** (−15% detection until first kill, then permanently revoked), **Bagman** (+1 loot slot, ×1.2 loot value), **Lookout** (+25% awareness cone), or **Demo** (+1 breach/C4 charge). Hotkeys: **R** restart · **C** crew · **M** mission select / Crew Report · **P** watch replay · **Esc** dismiss.

## 11. Facility Control & Access  *(new 2026-06-24 — Warehouse Job has all five)*

## 12. Crowd, Civilians & Panic  *(new 2026-06-25 — Office Hours is the best test bed, dense civilian clusters)*
- **Crowd Panic Propagation** — a civilian who witnesses a KO or a non-silenced gunshot near other civilians screams and flees; nearby civilians catch the scream and flee too, cascading outward (caps at 8 panicked). An armed guard caught inside a mob of 3+ panicking civilians drops focus to SEARCHING. No player input — trigger it by KO'ing near a crowd. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=crowd-panic`.
- **Slip the Crowd (Crowd Blend)** — walk WITH 3+ fleeing panicked civilians and your detection rate is slashed to ~18%; a green **◆ BLENDING** pip shows top-center. Sprinting against the crowd's flow, or the mob thinning below 3, breaks it immediately. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=slip-crowd`.
- **Fire-Alarm Evacuation (Evac Flood)** — crack a red fire panel (Hacker, hold **E** 2 s) to start a 45 s building-wide evacuation: every civilian streams to the nearest fire exit, and guards switch to escort/usher mode (vision cones off, radios cut) for the duration, then restore to normal patrol. *First test:* Warehouse 01's WarehouseFirePanel, upper floor x=600 — crack it with Vex. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=evac-flood`.
- **Civilian Escort** — hold **E** near a panicked (fleeing) civilian for 0.6 s to grab them as a living shield; armed guards refuse to fire while they're in the line of fire ("Let them go!"). Press **E** to release; sprinting (>100 px/s) auto-breaks the grab; movement speed is capped at 60% while holding them. Harming the civilian counts as a civilian KO against your rank. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=civ-escort`.
- **Calm the Panic** — the Medic presses **Q** within 80 px of a panicking civilian and stands completely still for 2 s to calm them: they return to PATROL and stop infecting nearby civilians (cancels their pending contagion hops). Moving cancels the channel. Non-Medics are denied. *Debug:* `--demo-feature=calm-civilian`.
