# Inspirations

Curated creative references for this game — the games, films, music, and people
you're reaching for, by category. The **Designer** reads this for tone/feel when
generating ideas and citing concerns.

**These are guidance, not rules.** They nudge; they don't override Philosophy
voice-fit or `must_not_include`. Most runs may draw on none of them — that's
fine. CEO-authored: this is optional — leave it as-is, fill it in, or delete it.
(The Designer reads it; it never writes to it.)

Format per entry: `- <Title> (<medium>) — <what to take from it>`
